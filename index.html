MICHIGAN MALAYSIAN STUDENTS ASSOCIATION

Welcome to MiMSA!

Michigan Malaysian Students Association (MiMSA) brings Malaysians together and is proud to have a close-knit community in Ann Arbor. We strive to promote Malaysian culture to the rest of the campus community. MiMSA is not only open to Malaysian students but also to other Malaysians living near the campus area and anyone who is interested in learning more about Malaysia.
